Medieval vs Modern Medicine

Made by Mustafa Yasir, Kiaan Khera, Marcus Han and Markus Wong

Medieval Medicine

  • Based on the theory of the four humours
  • Most people went to religion for hope and as because people thought the black death was a punishment from God
  • Used herbal remedies and bloodletting
  • Low hygiene, frequent diseases so people tried to avoid bad smells
  • Limited understanding of human body or germs

Modern Medicine

  • Scientific research and know well about cures for the human body
  • Advanced diagnostics and treatments like MRI and X-ray
  • Use of vaccines, antibiotics, and surgeries help to fight bacteria
  • Good hygiene, prevention of bacteria and medicine
  • Healthcare systems and many hospitals

Key Differences

  • Prevention: Religion vs. Science
  • Treatment: Herbal vs. Antibiotics
  • Hygiene: Poor vs. Rich
  • Life Expectancy: 30s-40s vs. 70s-80s
  • Tools: Scissors vs. X-ray
