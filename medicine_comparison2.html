Modern Medicine

Made by Mustafa yasir, Kiaan Khera, Marcus Han and Markus Wong

Introduction

Modern medicine is built on centuries of scientific advancement. It focuses on evidence-based treatment, diagnostics, and prevention. With technology, collaboration, and research, medicine today saves lives at a scale never seen before.

Key Developments

  • Vaccines: Eradicated deadly diseases like during the days of Covid.
  • Antibiotics: Revolutionized the treatment of bacterial infections so it can no longer spread.
  • Imaging: MRI, CT scans, and X-rays help doctors see inside the body safely.
  • Genetics: Personalised medicine and early detection of inherited conditions., help prevent and fight diseases.
  • Medicine: Doctors now treat patients remotely using tablets and AI tools.

Benefits of Modern Medicine

Life expectancy has increased drastically. Diseases once considered deadly are now manageable or even curable. Mental health and wellbeing are also gaining the attention they deserve. Clean hospitals, safe surgeries, and effective medication are now standard in many parts of the world.

Conclusion

Modern medicine continues to evolve with innovation. As we move into the future, artificial intelligence, robotics, and biotechnology will redefine how we care for human health.
